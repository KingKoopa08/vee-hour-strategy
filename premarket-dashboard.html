Core Metrics

  • Price & Change %
    Current pre-market price and percentage change from previous close. Green indicates gains, red indicates losses.
  • Volume
    Total shares traded during pre-market session. Higher volume indicates stronger interest and better liquidity for trading.
  • VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)
    The average price weighted by volume. Trading above VWAP suggests bullish sentiment, below suggests bearish.
